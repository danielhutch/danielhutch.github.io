About

I’m an Interaction Designer currently freelancing in London. I endeavour to make content accessible as possible whilst creating engaging experiences.

I’ve enjoyed time freelancing at the following agencies: AnalogFolk, AKQA Berlin, Bostock and Pollitt, Dare, Engage, Moving Brands, and Wilson Fletcher.

Over the past three years I have been developing and documenting digital standards for EE and HSBC.

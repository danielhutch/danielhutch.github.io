About

I’m an Interaction Designer currently freelancing in London with over 13 years in the industry. I endeavour to make content accessible as possible whilst creating engaging experiences.

Over the past three years I have been developing and documenting digital standards for EE and HSBC.

I’ve enjoyed time freelancing at the following agencies: AnalogFolk, AKQA Berlin, Bostock and Pollitt, Dare, Engage, Moving Brands, and Wilson Fletcher.

Previous clients include:

Apple, Barclaycard, EE, Google, HSBC, Invesco Perpetual, Kingston University, London Fashion Week, MSN, Nokia, Science Museum, Sony, Swisscom, The Royal Marsden, and Toshiba.

About

I’m an Interaction Designer currently freelancing in London. With over fourteen years of digital experience across multiple industries and at some of the best agencies.

Over the past five years I have been creating and documenting digital standards for HSBC and EE along with designing websites, platforms and services.

I enjoy leading teams, working with directly with clients and being as hands on as possible. With a keen interest in technology I endeavour to keep up to date with development best practices; I’m actively learning and building as much as I can. I also enjoy teaching and annually guest lecture at Kingston University on their UX postgraduate course.

Previous clients include: Apple, Barclaycard, EE, Google, HSBC, Invesco Perpetual, Kingston University, London Fashion Week, MSN, Nokia, Science Museum, Sony, Swisscom, The Royal Marsden NHS Foundation Trust.

Agencies include: AnalogFolk, AKQA Berlin, Bostock and Pollitt, Dare, Engage, Moving Brands, and Wilson Fletcher.
